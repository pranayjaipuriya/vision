About Us

This app improves the communication experience of people with partial or total hearing loss problems and people with disrupted speech.

The app provides 24/7 real-time captioning (with up to 95% accuracy, based on artificial intelligence), on your smartphone.

The app can be used for social interactions such as quickly talking with colleagues at work or internal meetings.

Companion can also be used to instantly transcribe what people say in classroom trainings, business meetings, and many more.

**Features**

  • live transcribe
  • super easy to use and intuitive
  • converts speech to text and text to speech
  • record the conversation in text and provides access to history for later use
